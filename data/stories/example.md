# The Adventure of the Cigar Ash (sample script)

Format: one segment per line — `[Speaker|tone] dialogue`. story_agent parses these into indexed
segments; only the index crosses the bus, the connector renders each in the character's voice.

[Narrator|calm] It was a foggy evening on Baker Street.
[Sherlock|analytical] Watson, observe the ash upon the windowsill.
[Watson|astonished] Good heavens, Holmes — I see nothing remarkable!
[Sherlock|amused] Precisely. Its absence is the very clue.
[Narrator|calm] And with that, the game was afoot.
